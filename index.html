About Me

I'm a professional software developer specialising in backend systems. I started my career at UBS before joining Football Radar in 2015.

At Football Radar I've mostly worked on building predictive models for clients in the sports betting industry. I have experience working with AWS to build out backtesting infrastructure and CUDA to enable match simulations in real-time.

In recent years I've enjoyed taking on a leadership role and managing a team. Although I still love programming, I've had great fun getting more involved with recruitment, system architecture and mentoring members of the team.

My Interests

My keenness for rock climbing is unfortunately not matched by any quantity of natural talent. However, I'd heartily endorse it as a brilliant hobby and it forms a cornerstone of my social life. I'm also an enthusiastic skier.

In addition to technology, where I do my best to keep abreast of the latest fads, I enjoy reading about finance and anything remotely mechanical in nature.

I married Katie in 2018. We moved to the Kentish countryside in 2020. At time of writing, the novelty of having donkeys, lambs and alpacas on our doorstep has yet to wear off.
